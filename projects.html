the mudita project is a rails app that allows user to share and find charitable acts of kindness near them. Users can add charitable acts to their "completed" list or their "to-do" list and allows them to see their moral growth through a beautiful minimalist profile. Users can befriend other fellow do-gooders and interact with each other through a news feed for friends only as well as a newsfeed showing what everyone is up to.



RealTime

RealTime is an iOS app that helps college students find and post local pop-up events. Users can browse events near them through the news feed and filter by events that are happening now or in 24 hours time. Additionally students can filter by categories such as "food", "nightlife", "innovation" etc to find events pertinent to them. Users can also create new events that they happen to find. (programmed in Xcode in Swift with Parse database)
